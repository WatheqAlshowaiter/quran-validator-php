# Quran Dataset Notice

This package bundles 6,236 Hafs verses across 114 surahs in Uthmani and Imlaei-derived forms.

Source: [QUL (Quranic Universal Library)](https://qul.tarteel.ai/) by [Tarteel AI](https://tarteel.ai/), specifically its [Uthmani ayah-by-ayah](https://qul.tarteel.ai/resources/quran-script/88) and [Imlaei simple](https://qul.tarteel.ai/resources/quran-script/84) resources. The data was obtained through the inspected `yazinsai/quran-validator` and `yazinsai/quran-validator-py` distributions, which identify the same source.

QUL states that resources are intended to be downloaded and packaged with projects. QUL also states that individual resources vary in copyright status and may require separate attribution or restrictions. The resource pages inspected did not display an additional resource-specific license grant.

Accordingly:

- Attribution to QUL and Tarteel AI is preserved here.
- The package's MIT `LICENSE` applies to original PHP source, not by implication to this dataset.

- Corrections should be checked against the authoritative source and reported upstream.
